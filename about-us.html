OUR STORY

Our platform is designed to provide complete transparency so you can make confident decisions before booking. From boys' hostels and girls' hostels to student accommodation near colleges, universities, coaching institutes, and workplaces, Looh Hostels offers a wide range of verified options to meet different needs. Every listing includes useful details such as room types, rent, facilities, security features, meal availability, and nearby landmarks.

At Looh Hostels, we are committed to making hostel searching easier, faster, and more reliable. Our goal is to help every user find a place that feels safe, comfortable, and welcoming while giving hostel owners a trusted platform to reach genuine tenants. Whether you're moving to Patna for education, work, or a fresh start, Looh Hostels is here to make your accommodation journey simple and stress-free.

LOOH started as a simple, stubborn idea: what if finding a verified hostel took the same effort as booking a train ticket — one search, real prices, no one standing between the student and the room?

We built it first for Patna, because that's the ground we know best — Golghar to Kankarbagh, every gallis that hides a good hostel behind a bad signboard. Now we're carrying the same promise to every city students move to.
