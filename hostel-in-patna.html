The Ultimate Guide to Finding a Hostel in Patna

Moving to a new city for your studies is a massive step. Finding the right accommodation is the foundation of your success. If your living space is noisy, unsafe, or too far from your coaching institute, your studies will suffer. That is why choosing the right student hostel in Patna requires careful consideration of location, pricing, facilities, and community.

1. Top Locations for Students in Patna

Location is everything. The closer you are to your educational center, the more time and energy you save. Here are the prime locations where we offer highly-rated accommodations:

  • Boring Road: Widely considered the educational heart of Patna, Boring Road is packed with premier IIT-JEE and NEET coaching institutes. A hostel here means you are walking distance from your classes, cafes, and libraries. It is a highly vibrant, student-friendly area.
  • Rajendra Nagar: Known for its peaceful environment and historical significance in education, Rajendra Nagar is ideal for serious aspirants. It offers excellent connectivity and a quieter atmosphere conducive to deep study.
  • Danapur & RPS More: As Patna expands, Danapur and the RPS More area have become significant hubs for engineering and medical students. Hostels in this region often feature modern infrastructure and slightly larger living spaces.
  • Kankarbagh: One of Asia's largest colonies, Kankarbagh is home to numerous competitive exam preparation centers. It offers excellent healthcare facilities, parks, and diverse housing options.

  • Nutritious, Home-Style Meals: Good health drives good grades. Our partnered hostels provide hygienic, balanced meals (Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner) catering to student dietary needs.

3. Transparent Pricing & Zero Brokerage

One of the biggest hurdles students face in Patna is dealing with middlemen who demand heavy commissions. At Looh Hostels, we have eliminated this entirely. Our platform operates on a strict zero-brokerage policy. The price you see on our app is the price you pay.

Pricing for a hostel in Patna generally varies based on the location and room configuration:

  • Triple/Four Sharing Rooms: Highly budget-friendly, typically ranging from Rs 5,000 to Rs 6,500 per month. Perfect for students looking to minimize costs while enjoying all core facilities.
  • Double Sharing Rooms: The most popular choice, offering a balance of privacy and affordability. These generally range from Rs 6,500 to Rs 8,000 per month.
  • Single/Private Rooms: For students who need absolute silence and privacy, single rooms are available starting from Rs 8,000 to Rs 12,000+ per month, depending on the luxury of the amenities provided.
